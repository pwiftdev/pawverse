Welcome to the park

Meet other animals, explore the island, and make your own stories. Here are the essentials before your paws hit the ground.

1 · Run free

Use WASD to move, Shift to sprint, and Space to jump. Move the mouse to look around.

Dogs press F to catch player cats and raccoons in three bites. Cats and raccoons should sprint, dodge, and escape.

Follow Pup Goals, sniff out discoveries with N, chat with Enter, and open the ? panel anytime for help.
